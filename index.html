About Adjentic

Adjentic brings over a decade of hands-on AWS and DevOps experience to organisations that need infrastructure built properly — not just advised on.

We've worked across banking, e-commerce, ticketing, telecoms, and enterprise software — environments where infrastructure failures have real consequences. That operational reality shapes how we approach every engagement: pragmatically, with an eye on what the team inherits when we're done.

Engagements range from focused advisory work to full hands-on delivery contracts. We work in small iterations with regular feedback — so you're never waiting weeks to find out if things are going in the right direction. No bloated project plans, no unnecessary complexity, no knowledge locked away when the engagement ends.

  • 10+ Years of DevOps & Cloud experience
  • AWS AWS background — DevOps Professional & Solutions Architect certified
  • UK Registered in England & Wales — remote-first, available nationally
